Research on Adversarial Examples Attack and Defense for Intelligent Speech Systems

Prof.Lipeng Song

By executing targeted attacks on neural networks and introducing subtle, challenging-to-detect perturbations, we create adversarial audio. This methodology is built upon the DeepSpeech deep learning open-source speech-to-text engine model, specifically designed to interfere with the target engine's capacity to accurately recognize audio. The ultimate goal is to manipulate the audio recognition process, ensuring that the model interprets the audio as the desired text.
